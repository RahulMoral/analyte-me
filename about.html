Who We Are

We're a team of professionals from around the world with diverse experience in analytical instrumentation. Our backgrounds span science, engineering, manufacturing, teaching, equipment distribution, service, and day-to-day instrument use. Over the years, we've all encountered the same problem: finding clear, accurate, and complete information about analytical equipment is harder than it should be.

This project was created to solve that. Drawing on our combined knowledge, we've built a platform that brings together essential data on instruments, configurations, applications, and services in one place. It's designed to be useful to both experts and newcomers, regardless of background or location.

We operate independently and our focus is on supporting users, research, education, and practical work in laboratories and industry.

Human to Human

Although our platform uses AI in many ways, its foundation is human experience. Science and technology depend on knowledge sharingânot just data, but actual insight from real use and real problems.

This platform aims to make that process easier. It's a tool to help people understand, compare, and work more effectively with analytical equipment by learning from each other.

Clear, Fair, and Care

All data here is based on open sources and reviewed for accuracy. We don't sell placements, run ads, or promote brands. We don't track users or monetize traffic. Our only priority is to maintain a reliable and easy-to-use database for the benefit of the community.

We're expanding.

New categories of instruments will be added regularly. The database of labs, service companies, training programs, and consumables suppliers will continue to grow.
